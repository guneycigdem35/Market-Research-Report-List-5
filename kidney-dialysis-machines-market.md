<p><p>The kidney dialysis machines market consists primarily of two types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis machines filter blood through an artificial kidney, typically in a clinical setting, while peritoneal dialysis uses the patient’s abdominal lining to filter waste, allowing for treatment at home. The choice between these methods depends on patient preference, medical condition, and lifestyle. As chronic kidney disease rates rise, the demand for both hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis solutions is increasing globally.</p></p>
