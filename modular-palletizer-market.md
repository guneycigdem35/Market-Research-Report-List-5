<p><p>The modular palletizer market is categorized mainly into three types: Inline Infeed, Sweep Infeed, and others. Inline Infeed systems allow products to be fed directly into the palletizer in a straight line, ensuring high efficiency and minimal floor space usage. Sweep Infeed systems utilize a sweeping mechanism to arrange products onto the pallet, ideal for varying packaging types. Other types may include robotic or hybrid solutions, offering versatility in handling diverse applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs and manufacturing setups.</p></p>
