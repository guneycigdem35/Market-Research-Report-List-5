<p><p>The COVID-19 antibody test market consists of three primary types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results using a simple process, making them ideal for point-of-care testing. ELISA involves a laboratory-based method that detects antibodies with high sensitivity and specificity. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit viral infection, providing insight into potential immunity levels. Each test type serves distinct purposes in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19 infection and immunity.</p></p>
