<p><strong>The Automotive Tire Carrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bumper Mounted Tire Carriers</li><li>Tailgate Reinforcement Style Tire Carriers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive tire carrier market is primarily divided into two types: bumper-mounted tire carriers and tailgate reinforcement style tire carriers. Bumper-mounted tire carriers are attached directly to the vehicle's rear bumper, providing a secure and accessible location for an extra tire. In contrast, tailgate reinforcement style tire carriers are designed to reinforce the tailgate, allowing for the mounting of a spare tire while enhancing the rear door's structural integrity. Both styles cater to off-road enthusiasts and provide practical solutions for spare tire storage.</p></p>
