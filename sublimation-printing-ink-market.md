<p><p>Sublimation printing ink types are tailored for various fabric materials. For polyester, the ink is specifically designed to bond with synthetic fibers, producing vibrant prints and durability. For polyester blends, the ink accommodates mixtures of polyester and other materials, ensuring good adherence and color retention. For cotton, specialized sublimation inks or pre-treatments are required, as cotton doesn't naturally bond with dye, allowing for high-quality prints with a softer feel, though not as vibrant as with synthetic fabrics.</p></p>
