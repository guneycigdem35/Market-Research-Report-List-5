<p><p>The Motorcycle Brake by Wire (BBW) system is categorized into single-channel and multiple-channel markets. A single-channel BBW system controls braking through one electronic channel, often simplifying design and reducing costs, but may compromise performance. In contrast, a multiple-channel BBW system utilizes several channels for independent braking control, enhancing safety and responsiveness by allowing for more precise modulation of braking force across different wheels. This advancement supports better handling and stability, catering to higher-performance motorcycles.</p></p>
