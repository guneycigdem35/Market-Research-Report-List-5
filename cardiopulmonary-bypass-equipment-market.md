<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump Cardiopulmonary Bypass Equipment</li><li>Double Roller Pump Cardiopulmonary Bypass Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass equipment market is categorized into two main types: single roller pump and double roller pump systems. Single roller pump cardiopulmonary bypass equipment utilizes a single roller mechanism to circulate blood during surgery, providing a simpler design and operation. In contrast, double roller pump equipment incorporates two rollers, allowing for more precise flow control and increased efficiency. These systems are essential for heart surgeries, supporting the patient's circulatory and respiratory functions while the heart is temporarily stopped.</p></p>
