<p><p>The PE protective film market includes High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Medium-Density Polyethylene (MDPE), and Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) films. HDPE films are rigid, durable, and resistant to chemicals, ideal for heavy-duty applications. MDPE films offer a balance of flexibility and strength, suitable for moderate protection needs. LDPE films are highly flexible, transparent, and easy to apply, making them perfect for lightweight, surface-sensitive materials. Each type caters to specific industrial requirements, ensuring optimal protection for surfaces during storage, transportation, or processing.</p></p>
