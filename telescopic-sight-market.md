<p><strong>The Telescopic Sight Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Collimating Optical Sight</li><li>Reflex Sight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telescopic sight market comprises various types, mainly including collimating optical sights and reflex sights. Collimating optical sights offer precision aiming by providing a magnified view of the target, typically used in long-range shooting or hunting. Reflex sights, on the other hand, utilize a reticle that projects onto a lens, allowing for faster target acquisition with both eyes open, commonly favored in tactical and competitive shooting scenarios. Both types enhance accuracy and visual engagement for users.</p></p>
