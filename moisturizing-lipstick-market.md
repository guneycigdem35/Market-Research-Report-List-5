<p><p>The moisturizing lipstick market can be categorized into two primary types: cream and fluid. They glide on smoothly, making them ideal for those seeking long-lasting moisture. In contrast, fluid lipsticks have a lightweight, liquid formulation that dries down to a more matte finish, yet still maintains hydration. These options cater to diverse preferences, balancing moisture with varying levels of coverage and finish.</p></p>
