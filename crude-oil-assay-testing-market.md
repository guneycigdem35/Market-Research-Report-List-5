<p><p>Crude oil assay testing encompasses various methods to analyze the composition and properties of crude oil. Total distillation testing evaluates the boiling range and yields of distillates, while freeze point testing determines the lowest temperature at which the oil remains liquid. Smoke point testing assesses the stability of kerosene in combustion, and total sulfur testing measures sulfur content to ensure compliance with environmental regulations. Other tests may include viscosity, density, and pour point analysis, providing essential data for refining and processing.</p></p>
