Natural gravity dehydration relies on the natural process of gravity, allowing water to drain away from solids, often used in wastewater treatment. Gravity concentration dehydration, on the other hand, enhances the separation process by utilizing gravitational forces to isolate denser materials from liquids, commonly employed in mining and mineral processing to improve resource recovery efficiency.</p></p>
