<p><p>Solar thermal collectors are essential components in solar energy systems, primarily classified into two main types: flat plate collectors and evacuated tube collectors. Flat plate collectors feature a simple design with a flat, insulated box containing a dark absorber plate, ideal for moderate climates. In contrast, evacuated tube collectors consist of rows of glass tubes that maintain a vacuum, enhancing efficiency in colder environments. Both types cater to diverse applications, from residential water heating to industrial processes, driving growth in the solar thermal market.</p></p>
