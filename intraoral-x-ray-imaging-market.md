<p><p>The intraoral X-ray imaging market comprises several key types, including bitewing, periapical, and occlusal X-rays. Bitewing X-rays are used to detect cavities between teeth and assess the supporting bone. Periapical X-rays provide a comprehensive view of an entire tooth, including its surrounding structures, aiding in diagnosis of various dental issues. Occlusal X-rays capture the entire arch of teeth in one image, facilitating the assessment of occlusion and the relationship of teeth within the dental arch.</p></p>
