<p><p>The membrane disc diffuser market is segmented into coarse bubble and fine bubble types, each catering to different aeration needs in applications like wastewater treatment and aquaculture. Coarse bubble membrane diffusers generate larger air bubbles, providing rapid oxygen transfer and mixing in less depth and are suitable for applications with high suspended solids. In contrast, fine bubble membrane diffusers produce smaller bubbles, enhancing oxygen dissolution efficiency, making them ideal for deeper tanks and delicate ecosystems requiring precise aeration control.</p></p>
