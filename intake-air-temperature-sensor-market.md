<p><strong>The Intake-Air Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Into Manifold Absolute Pressure Sensor</li><li>Standalone Intake-Air Temperature Sensor</li><li>Integrated Into Mass Air Flow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intake-Air Temperature Sensor market comprises three main types: integrated into the Manifold Absolute Pressure (MAP) sensor, standalone, and integrated into the Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensor. Integrated MAP sensors combine temperature sensing with pressure measurement for optimized engine performance and efficiency. Standalone sensors provide independent temperature readings for precise air management. MAF-integrated sensors measure both air mass and temperature, delivering additional data for enhanced fuel injection and emissions control, driving advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
