<p><p>The high current common mode choke coils market consists of two primary types: through hole type and SMD (surface mount device) type. Through hole type chokes are designed for insertion into PCB holes, providing robust connections and higher current handling capabilities, making them suitable for extensive applications. In contrast, SMD type chokes are mounted directly onto the surface of PCBs, enabling compact designs and facilitating automated assembly processes. Both types are essential for suppressing electromagnetic interference in various electronic devices.</p></p>
