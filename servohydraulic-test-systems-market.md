<p><p>Servohydraulic test systems encompass two primary market types: dynamic cyclic testing and static testing. Dynamic cyclic testing involves subjecting materials or components to repeated loading and unloading cycles, simulating real-world conditions to assess fatigue and durability. In contrast, static testing measures the response of materials under constant loads, providing insights into strength and deformation characteristics. Both types are essential for evaluating material performance in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and construction, ensuring safety and reliability in engineering applications.</p></p>
