<p><p>The reactive waterproof coating market consists primarily of two types: liquid and dry reactive waterproof coatings. Liquid reactive waterproof coatings are typically used for seamless applications, offering flexibility and ease of application on various surfaces. They chemically bond with substrates to create a durable moisture barrier. In contrast, dry reactive waterproof coatings are applied in a powder form and require activation through moisture or other methods, providing robust protection in harsh environments. Both types serve critical roles in construction and maintenance.</p></p>
