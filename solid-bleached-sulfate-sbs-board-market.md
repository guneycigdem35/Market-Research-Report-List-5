<p><p>Solid Bleached Sulfate (SBS) board is primarily classified into two types: SBS C1S (Coated One Side) and SBS C2S (Coated Two Sides). SBS C1S features a smooth, coated surface on one side, making it ideal for applications such as high-quality printing and packaging where one side is visibly critical. In contrast, SBS C2S is coated on both sides, offering enhanced printability and durability, suitable for packaging that requires a premium finish and greater visual appeal. Both types are highly versatile in various industries.</p></p>
