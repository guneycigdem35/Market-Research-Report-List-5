<p><p>The radiant ceiling panels market is segmented into two main types: water heating and electric heating. Water heating panels utilize a network of pipes carrying hot water to radiate warmth across the ceiling, providing efficient and even heat distribution. Electric heating panels, on the other hand, use electric resistance to generate heat, offering ease of installation and control. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in residential and commercial settings, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency.</p></p>
