<p><p>Medical Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) is an advanced imaging technology that provides three-dimensional, high-resolution images of dental and maxillofacial structures. Unlike conventional computed tomography, CBCT utilizes a conical X-ray beam to capture images from various angles, which are then reconstructed into detailed 3D arrays. This technology is particularly beneficial in dentistry and orthodontics for precise diagnoses, treatment planning, and surgical guidance.</p><p>The Medical Cone Beam Computed Tomography Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period, driven by increasing incidence of dental and orthopedic diseases, advancements in imaging technologies, and rising demand for minimally invasive surgeries.
