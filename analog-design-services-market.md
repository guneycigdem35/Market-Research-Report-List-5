<p><p>Analog design services encompass two primary market types: forward design and reverse design. </p><p>Forward design involves creating new analog circuits or systems from initial specifications, focusing on innovation and tailored solutions for specific applications. </p><p>In contrast, reverse design entails analyzing and reproducing existing analog circuitry, often to enhance performance or reduce costs. This approach is crucial for understanding legacy systems, ensuring compatibility, and facilitating upgrades. Both markets play vital roles in advancing technology and meeting diverse client needs in the analog domain.</p></p>
