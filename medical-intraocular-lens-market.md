<p><p>The intraocular lens market features several types designed to correct vision post-cataract surgery. Monofocal lenses provide clear vision at one distance, typically used for distance vision. Multifocal lenses offer multiple vision zones for near and far sight. Toric lenses are specialized for astigmatism, providing clear vision at all distances. Extended depth of field lenses enhance focus range, minimizing the need for glasses. Each type caters to specific visual needs, improving patients' quality of life after surgery.</p></p>
