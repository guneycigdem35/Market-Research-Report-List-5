<p><p>The automotive in-vehicle air purifier market comprises three main types: air purifiers, air ionizers, and hybrid systems. Air purifiers utilize HEPA filters to capture airborne particles, improving air quality. Air ionizers release negatively charged ions that attract and neutralize pollutants, enhancing freshness. Hybrid systems combine the technologies of purifiers and ionizers, offering comprehensive air cleaning by filtering particles and reducing odors simultaneously. These products cater to increasing consumer demand for healthier in-vehicle environments and enhanced driving comfort.</p></p>
