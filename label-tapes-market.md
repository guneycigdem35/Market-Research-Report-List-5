<p><p>Label tapes are used for printing labels in various industries. They come in two main types: non-adhesive and adhesive. Non-adhesive label tapes are plain, requiring additional adhesive or fasteners for application, making them reusable and versatile. Adhesive label tapes come with a pre-applied sticky backing, offering convenience for permanent or semi-permanent labeling. Both types cater to different needs, with non-adhesive tapes ideal for temporary use and adhesive tapes suited for long-term labeling in retail, logistics, and industrial applications.</p></p>
