<p><p>The specialty chemical packaging market features several key types, including drums, intermediate bulk containers (IBCs), flexitanks, and other forms. Drums, typically made of plastic or metal, are widely used for smaller volumes. IBCs provide larger capacity storage and transport capabilities, ideal for bulk chemicals. Flexitanks are flexible bladder containers used for transporting liquids in standard shipping containers, optimizing space. Other packaging options may include bags, boxes, and custom containers, catering to specific chemical properties and supply chain requirements.</p></p>
