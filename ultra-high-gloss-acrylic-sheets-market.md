<p><p>The Ultra High Gloss Acrylic Sheets market primarily consists of two types: extruded acrylic sheets and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced through a continuous process, offering uniform thickness and lower cost, making them suitable for large applications. In contrast, cast acrylic sheets are manufactured by pouring acrylic resin into molds, resulting in superior optical clarity and impact resistance. This makes cast sheets ideal for high-end applications requiring durability and aesthetics, such as signage, displays, and architectural elements.</p></p>
