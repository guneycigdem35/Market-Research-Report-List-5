<p><p>The fire retardant fabric market consists of two main types: inherent fire retardant fabric and treated fire retardant fabric. Inherent fire retardant fabric is made from fibers that are naturally resistant to flames, maintaining their fire-resistant properties throughout the fabric's life. Conversely, treated fire retardant fabric is made from regular fibers that undergo a chemical treatment to enhance their flame resistance. Both types are essential for various applications, including protective clothing, upholstery, and industrial uses, ensuring safety in hazardous environments.</p></p>
