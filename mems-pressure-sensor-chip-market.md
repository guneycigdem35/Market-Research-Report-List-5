<p><p>The MEMS pressure sensor chip market features several key types. The pressure resistance type relies on changes in electrical resistance due to deformation from pressure, making it suitable for various applications. The piezoelectric type generates an electrical charge in response to applied pressure, ideal for dynamic pressure measurements. Other types may include capacitive sensors that detect pressure variations through changes in capacitance, or hybrid technologies that combine elements from different types, enhancing performance and versatility in various industrial applications.</p></p>
