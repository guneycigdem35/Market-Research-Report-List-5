<p><p>The construction market for adhesives and sealants comprises two main types: sealants and adhesives. Sealants are used to fill gaps and joints, preventing water, air, and dust infiltration, thereby enhancing durability and energy efficiency. Common materials include silicone, polyurethane, and acrylic. Adhesives, on the other hand, bond materials together, ensuring structural integrity and stability. They are essential in applications such as flooring, wall panels, and roofing. Both products play a crucial role in improving construction quality and longevity.</p></p>
