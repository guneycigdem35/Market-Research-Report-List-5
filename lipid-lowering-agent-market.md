Fibrates, such as fenofibrate and gemfibrozil, primarily target triglyceride levels and can raise HDL cholesterol. Other agents include omega-3 fatty acids, statins, and PCSK9 inhibitors, each offering unique mechanisms and effectiveness in treating dyslipidemia and reducing cardiovascular risks.</p></p>
