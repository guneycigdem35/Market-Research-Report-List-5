<p><p>The automotive automatic transmission (AT) system market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)** offers seamless gear shifting for improved fuel efficiency and smooth driving. </p><p>2. **Hydraulic Automatic Transmission (Hydraulic AT)** employs fluid pressure for gear changes, providing reliable performance and ease of use. </p><p>3. **Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT)** combines the efficiency of manual and automatic systems, employing two separate clutches for quick gear shifts, enhancing acceleration and overall driving dynamics. Each type caters to distinct consumer preferences and vehicle performance needs.</p></p>
