<p><p>Synthetic pheromones are artificially created chemical compounds that mimic natural pheromones, which are substances used by organisms, particularly insects, to communicate and influence behavior. They are also utilized in other industries, such as animal husbandry and human behavior studies.</p><p>The Synthetic Pheromones Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period. The agriculture sector remains the largest consumer of synthetic pheromones, with applications in crop protection and integrated pest management (IPM) programs.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include advancements in pheromone formulation technologies, such as microencapsulation, which enhances their effectiveness and longevity.
