<p><p>The Automatic Pneumatic Tourniquet market is segmented into two main types: Single Channel and Dual Channel. A Single Channel Tourniquet operates with one inflation line, simplifying use for basic procedures while ensuring adequate pressure management. In contrast, Dual Channel Tourniquets feature two independent inflation lines, allowing simultaneous control of multiple limbs or sections, enhancing versatility and safety in more complex surgeries. Both types are integral to modern surgical practices, improving patient outcomes through precise blood flow management.</p></p>
