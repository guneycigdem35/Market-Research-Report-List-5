<p><ul><li>Electrical Exit Sign</li><li>Non-electrical Exit Sign</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Exit Sign market is divided into two main types: electrical and non-electrical exit signs. Electrical exit signs are illuminated using LED or fluorescent lights, ensuring high visibility in low-light conditions and often featuring battery backup for power outages. Non-electrical exit signs, on the other hand, rely on photoluminescent materials or reflective surfaces, which absorb and emit light without requiring an external power source. Both types play a crucial role in safety and compliance within buildings by guiding occupants during emergencies.</p></p>
