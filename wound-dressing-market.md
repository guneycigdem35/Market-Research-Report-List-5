<p><p>The wound dressing market comprises two main types: traditional and advanced wound dressings. In contrast, advanced wound dressings incorporate innovative materials and technologies like hydrogels, bioactive dressings, and antimicrobial agents, designed to promote faster healing, enhance moisture retention, and reduce infection risk.
