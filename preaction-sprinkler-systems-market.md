<p><p>Preaction sprinkler systems are crucial fire protection solutions that operate in different configurations: Non-Interlock, Single Interlock, and Double Interlock. </p><p>Non-Interlock systems activate water flow upon detection of heat or smoke, offering rapid response. Single Interlock requires the activation of the fire alarm system and detects heat before releasing water, minimizing accidental discharge. Double Interlock systems ensure both the detection of heat and activation of the alarm before water is released, providing the highest level of protection against false alarms while safeguarding sensitive areas.</p></p>
