In contrast, inorganic binders are composed of mineral-based materials, offering high thermal stability and strength, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, including ceramics, construction, and manufacturing, tailored to specific performance requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
