<p><p>The enema-based devices market encompasses various types designed for specific therapeutic purposes. Cleansing enemas are primarily used for bowel preparation and detoxification. Carminative enemas help relieve gas and bloating by introducing soothing substances. Retention enemas are designed to be held in the bowel for a duration to enhance nutrient absorption or medication effectiveness. Return-flow enemas facilitate the removal of gas and stool by allowing fluid to flow in and out. Other variations may include specialized formulations or delivery methods tailored for unique medical conditions.</p></p>
