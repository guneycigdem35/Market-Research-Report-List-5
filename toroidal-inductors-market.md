<p><p>The toroidal inductors market comprises various types, including toroidal coils, common mode inductors, and SMT (Surface Mount Technology) inductors. Toroidal coils feature a doughnut-shaped core that provides low electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Common mode inductors help suppress noise in power lines, offering enhanced electromagnetic compatibility. SMT inductors are compact and designed for surface mounting in electronic circuits, catering to the demand for miniaturization in modern devices. Each type serves distinct roles in electronic components.</p></p>
