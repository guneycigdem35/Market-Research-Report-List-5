<p><p>Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) batteries are a type of rechargeable lead-acid battery that are sealed and do not require regular maintenance, making them more convenient for various applications. These batteries are designed with safety valves that regulate internal pressure, preventing the escape of gases while enabling gas recombination within the battery. As a result, they are popular in numerous sectors, including telecommunications, uninterruptible power supply (UPS), automotive, and renewable energy applications.</p><p>The VRLA battery market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy storage solutions and a shift towards renewable energy sources. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period, fueled by advancements in battery technology and the growing adoption of electric vehicles. Additionally, the rising need for efficient backup power systems in both residential and commercial sectors is propelling market growth.</p><p>Latest trends include innovations in battery chemistry and designs, improvement in energy density, and a focus on sustainability with the development of more eco-friendly lead-acid batteries.
