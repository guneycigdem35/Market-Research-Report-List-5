<p><p>The Anti-reflective (AR) and Anti-fingerprint (AF) nanocoating market can be categorized based on the heating methods used during their application: Resistance Heating and Electron Beam Heating. Resistance Heating utilizes electrical resistance to generate heat, allowing for uniform coating application on surfaces. In contrast, Electron Beam Heating employs focused electron beams to achieve precise heating, promoting faster curing and adhesion of coatings. Both methods enhance the performance and durability of nanocoatings, catering to various industries, including electronics and optics.</p></p>
