<p><p>The plastic ball bearings market comprises various types, primarily including radial ball bearings and deep groove ball bearings. Deep groove ball bearings, known for their versatile design, effectively handle both radial and axial loads, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Other market segments may include angular contact ball bearings and self-aligning ball bearings, catering to specific applications requiring unique load handling characteristics or environmental resistance.</p></p>
