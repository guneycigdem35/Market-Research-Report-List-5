<p><strong>The Rosin Cored Solder Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inactive Rosin</li><li>Rosin Activated (RA)</li><li>Rosin Mildly Activated (RMA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rosin-cored solder wire market comprises three main types: Inactive Rosin, Rosin Activated (RA), and Rosin Mildly Activated (RMA). Inactive Rosin has no activating agents and is typically used in applications where minimal flux activity is needed. RA contains more aggressive flux activators, enhancing solder joint reliability, ideal for diverse electronic repairs. RMA sits between the two, offering moderate activation for sensitive components, providing cleaner joints without excessive residue, making it suitable for a range of electronic soldering tasks.</p></p>
