<p><p>The sponge rubber materials market is categorized into three main types: closed-cell rubber, open-cell rubber, and others. Closed-cell rubber features a dense structure that prevents air and moisture penetration, making it ideal for insulation and buoyancy applications. Open-cell rubber has a more porous structure, allowing for enhanced breathability and cushioning, suitable for soundproofing and comfort. Other market categories may include specialized formulations like silicone or neoprene, catering to niche applications in automotive, medical, and construction industries.</p></p>
