<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>IP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video intercom devices are categorized into two main types: Analog and IP. Analog video intercoms utilize traditional wiring systems to transmit video and audio signals, making them cost-effective and simple to install. However, they generally offer limited features and lower video quality. In contrast, IP video intercoms leverage internet protocols, allowing for higher-resolution video, advanced functionality, and remote access via smartphones and computers. This modern approach supports integration with smart home systems, enhancing security and user experience.</p></p>
