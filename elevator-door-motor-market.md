<p><strong>The Elevator Door-motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inverter Door-motor</li><li>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Door-motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elevator door-motor market primarily comprises two types: Inverter Door-motors and Permanent Magnet Synchronous Door-motors. Inverter Door-motors use variable frequency drives to control motor speed and torque, enhancing energy efficiency and offering smoother operation. Permanent Magnet Synchronous Door-motors, on the other hand, utilize permanent magnets for rotation, providing high efficiency and compact design. Both types cater to the growing demand for advanced, energy-efficient elevator systems, enhancing safety and accessibility in modern buildings.</p></p>
