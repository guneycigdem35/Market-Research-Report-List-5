<p><p>The Automotive Air Flow Sensor market includes two primary types: Vane Meter Type and Hot Wire Type. Vane Meter sensors measure air intake using a mechanical flap that moves proportionally to airflow, offering durability and simplicity. Hot Wire sensors use a heated wire to detect airflow changes based on cooling effects, providing higher accuracy and faster response. Both types are crucial for optimizing engine performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions control, catering to different vehicle requirements and technological advancements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
