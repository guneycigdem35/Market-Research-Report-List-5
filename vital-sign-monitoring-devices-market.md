<p><p>The vital sign monitoring devices market includes various key segments such as heart rate monitors, pulse oximeters, and blood pressure monitors. Heart rate monitors track the number of heartbeats per minute, providing insights into cardiovascular health. Pulse oximeters measure oxygen saturation in the blood, indicating respiratory function. Blood pressure monitors assess the force of blood against artery walls, crucial for diagnosing hypertension and other conditions. Collectively, these devices enhance patient monitoring and enable timely medical interventions across healthcare settings.</p></p>
