<p><ul><li>Object Square Telephoto Lens</li><li>Bi-Telecentric Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telecentric lens market includes specialized types like object square telephoto lenses and bi-telecentric lenses. Object square telephoto lenses are designed to provide high magnification with minimal perspective distortion, making them ideal for precision applications in measurement and inspection. Bi-telecentric lenses maintain telecentric characteristics on both the object and image sides, ensuring consistent magnification and reduced optical aberrations. These lenses are extensively utilized in machine vision, semiconductor inspection, and other fields requiring high accuracy and uniformity in imaging.</p></p>
