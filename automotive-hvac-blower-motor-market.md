<p><p>The automotive HVAC blower motor market is divided into two main types: brushed and brushless motors. Brushed motors use carbon brushes to conduct electricity, offering simple designs and cost-effectiveness, but may have shorter lifespans due to brush wear. In contrast, brushless motors utilize electronic commutation, enhancing efficiency, durability, and reducing noise. As automakers emphasize fuel efficiency and quiet operation, brushless motors are increasingly favored in modern vehicles, driving growth in this segment of the HVAC market.</p></p>
