VFA are typically used in industrial applications, while MCFA, derived from coconut and palm oils, are increasingly popular in food and health products for their rapid metabolism. LCFA, commonly found in vegetable oils, are widely utilized in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals for their versatile properties and stability.</p></p>
