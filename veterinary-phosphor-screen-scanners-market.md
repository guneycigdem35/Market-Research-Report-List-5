<p><p>Veterinary phosphor screen scanners are advanced imaging devices used in veterinary medicine to capture high-resolution digital radiographs. These scanners utilize storage phosphor plates, which are exposed to X-rays and then scanned to produce detailed images of animals' internal structures. They are widely used in veterinary clinics and hospitals for diagnosing fractures, tumors, and other conditions in animals. The technology offers advantages such as reduced radiation exposure, improved image quality, and efficient workflow compared to traditional film-based radiography.</p><p>The Veterinary Phosphor Screen Scanners Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
