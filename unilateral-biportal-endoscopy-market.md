<p><p>Unilateral Biportal Endoscopy (UBE) is a minimally invasive surgical technique primarily used in orthopedic procedures, particularly for spinal surgeries. This approach utilizes two portals for the endoscope and working instruments, allowing surgeons to visualize and treat spinal pathologies effectively with reduced tissue damage and faster recovery times. The technique has gained traction due to its ability to minimize complications associated with traditional open surgeries, such as infection and blood loss, while maintaining surgical efficacy.</p><p>The Unilateral Biportal Endoscopy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4% during the forecast period.
