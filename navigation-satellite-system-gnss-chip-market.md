<p><strong>The Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Precision</li><li>Standard Precision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS chip market is divided into two main types: high precision and standard precision. High precision chips, often used in applications like surveying, agriculture, and autonomous vehicles, provide accuracy within centimeters. They incorporate advanced techniques such as multi-frequency signals and real-time kinematic (RTK) positioning. In contrast, standard precision chips, suitable for consumer electronics and basic navigation, offer accuracy within a few meters. These chips typically utilize single-frequency signals, making them cost-effective while meeting general navigation needs.</p></p>
