<p><p>Real Time Location Systems (RTLS) enable the tracking and management of assets and personnel in real time across various environments. Key market types include RFID, which uses radio waves for tracking; Wi-Fi, leveraging existing networks for location determination; Ultrasound, which employs sound waves for precise tracking; Infrared, using light waves for line-of-sight detection; Zigbee, a low-power protocol for device communication; and Ultra Wide Band (UWB), known for its high accuracy and capacity for complex environments.</p></p>
