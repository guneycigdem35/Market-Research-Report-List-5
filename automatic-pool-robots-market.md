<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li><li>Self-propelled (Floating)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool robot market is categorized into three main drive types: crawler drive, wheel drive, and self-propelled (floating). Crawler drive robots utilize tracks for enhanced maneuverability on various surfaces, making them ideal for uneven pool bottoms. Wheel drive robots employ wheels for efficient navigation, offering speed and simplicity in operation. Self-propelled robots float on the water's surface, using buoyancy for mobility, often featuring advanced navigation systems for effective cleaning without the need for physical attachments or guiding mechanisms.</p></p>
