<p><p>The biomedical temperature sensors market comprises several key types, each serving specific applications. Thermocouples provide rapid and wide temperature ranges, making them suitable for critical care. Thermistors offer high sensitivity and precision in a compact form, ideal for monitoring in medical devices. Fibre optic sensors enable remote temperature monitoring with immunity to electromagnetic interference. Infrared sensors allow non-contact temperature measurements for patient comfort. Lastly, liquid crystal temperature sensors provide visual cues, useful in patient care for easy temperature assessment.</p></p>
