<p><p>The seed coating market encompasses various types of products, including suspended seed coating agents, emulsions, wettable powders, and others. Suspended seed coating agents are liquid formulations that evenly distribute active ingredients on seeds. Emulsions combine oil and water-based components for enhanced adhesion and protection. Wettable powders are dry formulations that dissolve in water, allowing for efficient application, while other market types may include granules or films. Each type serves specific agricultural needs, improving seed performance and plant growth.</p></p>
