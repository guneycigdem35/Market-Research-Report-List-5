<p><p>Infrared thermal imaging systems can be categorized into two main types: hot type and quantum type. Hot type systems utilize solid-state sensors, typically designed for applications like building inspections and industrial monitoring due to their robustness and affordability. Conversely, quantum type systems employ advanced materials and technologies, such as cooled semiconductor detectors, offering higher sensitivity and resolution, making them suitable for security and military applications. Both types cater to various sectors, leveraging thermal detection for improved situational awareness and decision-making.</p></p>
