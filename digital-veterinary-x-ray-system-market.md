<p><ul><li>Computed Radiography</li><li>Direct Radiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Veterinary X-ray System market is segmented into two main types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Direct Radiography (DR). Computed Radiography uses a photostimulable phosphor plate to capture images, which are then digitally processed, offering flexibility and lower initial costs. In contrast, Direct Radiography captures images directly onto a digital receptor, providing immediate results and enhanced image quality. Both types play crucial roles in veterinary diagnostics, improving the efficiency and accuracy of animal care.</p></p>
