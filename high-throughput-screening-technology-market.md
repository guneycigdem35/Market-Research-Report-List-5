<p><p>The high-throughput screening technology market encompasses three main types: cell-based assays, lab-on-a-chip (LOC), and bioinformatics. Cell-based assays involve testing compounds on live cells to evaluate biological activity and toxicity. Lab-on-a-chip integrates multiple laboratory functions onto a single microfluidic chip, allowing for rapid and efficient analysis of biological samples. Bioinformatics supports high-throughput screening by providing computational tools and data analysis methods to interpret large datasets, facilitating drug discovery and development with enhanced precision and efficiency.</p></p>
