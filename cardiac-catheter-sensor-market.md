<p><ul><li>Pressure sensors</li><li>Temperature sensors</li><li>Biosensors</li><li>ECG sensors</li><li>Image sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac catheter sensor market encompasses various sensor types utilized during catheterization procedures. Pressure sensors measure hemodynamic parameters within the heart, assisting in diagnosing conditions. Temperature sensors monitor changes in blood temperature, which can indicate abnormalities. Biosensors detect specific biological markers to assess cardiac health. ECG sensors provide real-time electrocardiogram readings, aiding in arrhythmia detection. Image sensors capture visual information for detailed anatomical analysis, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and guiding interventions during cardiac procedures. Each sensor type plays a crucial role in improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
