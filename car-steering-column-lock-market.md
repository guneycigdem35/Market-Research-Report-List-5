<p><ul><li>Mechanical Steering Column Lock (MSCL)</li><li>Electronic Steering Column Lock (ESCL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Steering Column Lock market includes Mechanical Steering Column Locks (MSCL) and Electronic Steering Column Locks (ESCL). MSCL systems use physical mechanisms, such as keys and tumblers, to lock the steering wheel, preventing theft. ESCL systems, on the other hand, rely on electronic components like sensors and actuators, often integrated with vehicle immobilizers or keyless entry systems, offering enhanced security and convenience. Both types aim to secure vehicles, but ESCL provides advanced features like remote locking and integration with modern automotive technologies.</p></p>
