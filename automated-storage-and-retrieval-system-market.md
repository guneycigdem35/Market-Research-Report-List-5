<p><p>Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) are categorized into two primary types: Unit-Load AS/RS and Mini-Load AS/RS. Unit-Load AS/RS is designed for handling large, heavy items stored on pallets, ideal for warehouses and distribution centers needing efficient bulk storage and retrieval. In contrast, Mini-Load AS/RS is tailored for smaller components, often utilized in manufacturing and order processing environments, facilitating rapid access to a higher volume of goods. Both types enhance inventory management and operational efficiency.</p></p>
