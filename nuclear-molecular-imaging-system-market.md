<p><strong>The Nuclear Molecular Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positron Emission Tomography</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nuclear Molecular Imaging System market encompasses various technologies used for diagnostic imaging. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) offers detailed metabolic imaging by detecting gamma rays from positron emissions, highlighting abnormal tissue. Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) provides functional imaging by capturing emitted photons from radiotracers, allowing for three-dimensional visualization of organ function. Planar Scintigraphy Systems, a simpler form, create two-dimensional images using a single gamma camera, primarily employed for assessing the overall function of specific organs or detecting abnormalities.</p></p>
