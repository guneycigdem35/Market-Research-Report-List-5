<p><p>The Oil & Gas Subsea Umbilicals, Risers, and Flowlines (SURF) market consists of three key components: umbilicals, risers, and flowlines. Umbilicals are underwater cables that supply control and power to subsea equipment. Risers are vertical conduits that connect the seabed infrastructure to floating platforms, facilitating fluid transport. Flowlines are pipelines that transport oil and gas from subsea wells to processing facilities. Together, these elements form vital connections in subsea production systems, enabling efficient resource extraction.</p></p>
