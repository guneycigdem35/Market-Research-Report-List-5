<p><strong>The Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary-Screw Compressors</li><li>Reciprocating Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigeration and air conditioning compressor market is primarily segmented into rotary-screw compressors and reciprocating compressors. Rotary-screw compressors utilize two helical screws to compress refrigerant, offering higher efficiency and continuous operation, making them ideal for industrial applications. Conversely, reciprocating compressors function by using pistons to compress refrigerant, providing versatility and suitability for a wide range of cooling needs, including residential and commercial use. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining optimal temperature control across various sectors.</p></p>
