<p><p>The defoamer for inks market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I defoamers are primarily solvent-based, providing excellent performance in water-based ink formulations, effectively reducing foam during production. In contrast, Type II defoamers are silicone-based, known for their stability and compatibility with various ink systems. These types serve specific applications, with Type I being more cost-effective for high-volume production, while Type II offers enhanced efficiency and performance in demanding printing environments.</p></p>
