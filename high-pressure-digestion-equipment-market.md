Hotblock digestion relies on heated blocks to maintain consistent temperatures for sample treatment, suitable for various matrices. Other methods might include traditional high-pressure methods. These techniques are essential in analytical laboratories for preparing samples in environmental, food safety, and materials testing applications.</p></p>
