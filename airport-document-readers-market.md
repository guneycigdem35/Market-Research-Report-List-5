<p><p>The airport document readers market comprises two main types: desktop document readers and mobile document readers. Desktop document readers are typically used at fixed points like check-in counters and security checkpoints, providing high-speed, accurate scanning of passports and travel documents. In contrast, mobile document readers are portable devices used by airline personnel and border control agents, enabling on-the-go verification and enhancing operational flexibility. Both types play crucial roles in streamlining passenger processing and ensuring security in airport operations.</p></p>
