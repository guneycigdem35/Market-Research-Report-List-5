The Air Cushion Headbox utilizes air pockets to enhance pulp flow stability and reduce turbulence, leading to improved sheet quality. The Hydraulic Headbox employs precise hydraulic controls for consistent pulp delivery, enabling fine-tuning of flow rates and fiber orientation, thus optimizing production efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
