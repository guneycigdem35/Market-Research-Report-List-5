<p><p>The spectroscopy IR detector market is categorized into short wave, medium wave, and long wave infrared detectors. Short wave detectors typically operate in the 1-3 micron range, offering sensitivity for applications like gas analysis. Medium wave detectors, spanning 3-5 microns, are commonly used in thermal imaging and environmental monitoring. Long wave detectors, ranging from 8-14 microns, excel in detecting thermal radiation and are widely applied in security and military operations. Each type serves distinct applications based on wavelength sensitivity.</p></p>
