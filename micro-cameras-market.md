<p><p>The micro cameras market features various types, including NIR (Near-Infrared) and SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared) cameras, which cater to distinct applications. NIR cameras are designed to capture images in low-light conditions, primarily used in security, surveillance, and medical imaging. Conversely, SWIR cameras operate in the 1,000 to 2,500 nm wavelength range, enabling visibility through smoke, fog, and other obscurants, making them ideal for industrial and military use. Both types are pivotal in enhancing imaging capabilities across diverse sectors.</p></p>
