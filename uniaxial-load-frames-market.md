Electromechanical systems leverage motors and mechanical components for controlled loading, offering reliability and simplicity. Electrodynamic systems incorporate voice coil technology for dynamic loading applications, providing fast and accurate responses, ideal for testing materials under varying conditions. Each type serves distinct testing needs in materials research and quality control.</p></p>
