<p><p>The smart-tinting glass market comprises three main types: photochromic, electrochromic, and thermochromic. Photochromic glass automatically adjusts its tint in response to UV light, providing sun protection without manual intervention. Electrochromic glass changes color or transparency when an electric current is applied, allowing for controlled light and heat management. Thermochromic glass reacts to temperature changes, altering its tint to maintain comfort and reduce energy costs. Together, these technologies enhance energy efficiency and interior comfort in various applications.</p></p>
