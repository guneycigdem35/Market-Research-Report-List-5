<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis Thin Film Market is primarily divided into two types: Cellulose Thin Film and Composite Thin Film. Cellulose Thin Film membranes are made from natural cellulose-based materials, offering good permeability and moderate performance, but are less resistant to biofouling. In contrast, Composite Thin Film membranes consist of a thin layer of polyamide on a porous support, providing superior performance, increased resistance to fouling, and enhanced durability. These distinctions influence their applications in water treatment and purification processes.</p></p>
