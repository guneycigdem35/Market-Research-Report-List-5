<p><p>The flooring underlayment market comprises various types, each serving unique purposes. Cement Backer Units (CBU) provide a sturdy base for tile installations, enhancing moisture resistance. Polyethylene is a popular moisture barrier, often used for laminate and floating floors. Rubber underlayment offers excellent sound absorption and cushioning, making it ideal for acoustic applications. Cork is eco-friendly and provides thermal insulation, while plywood acts as a versatile base that adds stability and support to various flooring systems. Each type contributes to improved performance and longevity.</p></p>
