<p><p>The electrical fittings market can be categorized into metallic and non-metallic types. Metallic electrical fittings, made from materials like steel, aluminum, and copper, provide durability, strength, and better conductivity, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Non-metallic electrical fittings, crafted from plastics such as PVC and nylon, offer advantages like corrosion resistance and lightweight properties, suitable for residential and commercial use where electrical insulation is critical. Both types serve essential roles in electrical installations, emphasizing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
