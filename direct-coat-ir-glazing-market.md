<p><p>The Direct-Coat IR Glazing market is categorized into two main types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer glazing consists of a single layer that reflects infrared energy while allowing visible light to pass through, enhancing energy efficiency. In contrast, multilayer glazing features multiple coated layers, providing superior thermal insulation and enhanced performance against heat loss. Both types serve to improve energy efficiency in buildings, but multilayer products typically offer advanced capabilities for extreme climates and applications requiring greater energy control.</p></p>
