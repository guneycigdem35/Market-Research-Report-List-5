<p><p>The coin battery market includes several key types, each with unique characteristics. Lithium button coin batteries (CR) are rechargeable and offer high energy density, making them ideal for electronics. Silver oxide batteries (SR) provide stable voltage and are commonly used in watches and hearing aids. Alkaline button coin batteries (LR) are cost-effective and suited for low-drain devices. Zinc-air batteries leverage oxygen from the air for power, primarily used in hearing aids, providing high energy output and efficiency.</p></p>
