<p><p>The MOCVD susceptors market primarily features two types: CVD SiC coated graphite susceptors and CVI SiC coated graphite susceptors. CVD SiC coated susceptors are manufactured using Chemical Vapor Deposition, offering enhanced thermal stability and superior resistance to oxidation. In contrast, CVI SiC coated susceptors utilize Chemical Vapor Infiltration, leading to a more uniform and dense SiC layer. Both types are essential for optimized performance in MOCVD processes, particularly in industries like semiconductor fabrication and LED manufacturing, where precise temperature control is crucial.</p></p>
