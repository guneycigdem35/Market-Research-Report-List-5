<p><ul><li>Thermally Conductive Insulating Plastic</li><li>Thermally And Electrically Conductive Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermally conductive plastics market features two primary types: thermally conductive insulating plastics, which facilitate heat dissipation while preventing electrical conductivity, making them ideal for electronics and thermal management applications; and thermally and electrically conductive plastics, which enable both heat and electrical conduction, suitable for applications requiring conductivity, such as electromagnetic shielding and static electricity management. These materials are essential in industries like automotive, consumer electronics, and telecommunications, where efficient thermal management and electrical safety are crucial.</p></p>
