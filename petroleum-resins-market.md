<p><ul><li>C5 resins</li><li>C9 resins</li><li>C5/C9 resins</li><li>Hydrogenated resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The petroleum resins market comprises several key types: C5 resins, derived from the C5 fraction of crude oil and known for their low molecular weight, are used in adhesives and coatings. C9 resins, sourced from the C9 fraction, offer higher molecular weights and thermal stability, making them suitable for inks and sealants. C5/C9 resins combine properties of both types, enhancing flexibility and adhesion. Hydrogenated resins are modified for improved durability and low volatility, finding applications in high-performance coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
