<p><p>The DNA gene chip market includes two main types: cDNA (complementary DNA) and oligonucleotide DNA (oDNA). cDNA chips use synthesized complementary DNA to analyze gene expression levels, making them vital for understanding mRNA variations. In contrast, oligonucleotide chips utilize short sequences of DNA for precise hybridization with target sequences, allowing for genotyping and mutation detection. Both types play essential roles in genomics research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine by enabling high-throughput analysis of genetic material.</p></p>
