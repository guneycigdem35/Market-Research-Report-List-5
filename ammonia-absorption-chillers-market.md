<p><p>Ammonia absorption chillers operate using several key components: </p><p>1. **Evaporator**: Absorbs heat, causing ammonia to evaporate and cool the environment.</p><p>2. **Absorber**: Permanently holds the ammonia vapor and combines it with a refrigerant, usually water, to create a solution.</p><p>3. **Pump**: Circulates the ammonia-water solution through the system.</p><p>4. **Generator**: Heats the solution to separate ammonia from water for reuse.</p><p>5. **Analyzer**: Monitors system efficiency and performance.</p><p>6. **Others**: Includes ancillary components impacting overall operation.</p></p>
