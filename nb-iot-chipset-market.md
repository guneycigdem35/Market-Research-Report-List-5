<p><strong>The NB IoT Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>In-Band</li><li>Guard Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NB IoT chipset market is categorized into three types: Standalone, In-Band, and Guard Band. Standalone operates independently on dedicated spectrum, providing reliable performance for IoT applications. In-Band utilizes existing LTE spectrum, allowing efficient integration with current networks while leveraging their infrastructure. Guard Band, on the other hand, fits into the frequency gaps between LTE channels, maximizing spectrum utilization without disrupting ongoing services. Together, these types enable diverse deployment strategies for NB IoT technology across various environments.</p></p>
