<p><p>The Skeletal Deformation Correction market comprises various types, including osteotomy, limb reconstruction, arthrodesis, arthroplasty, and vertebral column resection. Osteotomy involves realigning bones to correct deformities. Limb reconstruction focuses on restoring limb function and alignment, often after trauma. Arthrodesis is a procedure that fuses joints to alleviate pain. Arthroplasty is the surgical repair or replacement of joints, enhancing mobility. Vertebral column resection addresses spinal deformities by removing segments of the vertebrae, improving alignment and function.</p></p>
