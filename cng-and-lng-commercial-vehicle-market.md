<p><p>CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) and LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) commercial vehicles are alternative fuel-powered vehicles designed to reduce emissions and operating costs compared to traditional diesel or gasoline-powered vehicles. These vehicles are widely used in transportation sectors such as logistics, public transit, and heavy-duty applications due to their environmental and economic benefits.</p><p>The CNG and LNG commercial vehicle market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing environmental regulations, rising fuel costs, and the push for cleaner energy solutions. The market is also witnessing collaborations between automakers and energy companies to enhance the availability and accessibility of CNG and LNG fuels.</p></p>
